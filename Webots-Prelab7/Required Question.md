The node is used as a drive controller that connects ROS2 and the Webots simulation. It subscribes to the /cmd_vel topic, which publishes Twist messages containing velocity commands for the robot. The linear.x and angular.z fields are read by the node, which converts them into velocities for the right and left wheels. The wheels can only move forward and backward or right and left, and the robot balances the velocities of each wheel to achieve the desired movement. To have the velocity commands and robot mechanics align, the step() function is used in the Webots controller to update every timestep of the simulation.
